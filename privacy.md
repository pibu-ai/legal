# PIBU Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 14, 2026

PIBU is a skincare ingredient analysis app operated by BYI Corp. This Privacy Policy explains what information PIBU collects, how we use it, when it may be shared, and the choices you have.

PIBU does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Ingredient and skin-fit information is for informational and educational use only.

## Information We Collect

We collect information you provide or generate while using PIBU, including:

- Account information: Google sign-in email address, display name, profile image URL, app language, and authentication provider.
- Profile information: skin type, sensitive-skin setting, profile setup status, and preferred or avoided ingredient preferences.
- Scan and product activity: skincare product scans, product-open history, recent scan history, saved products, watchlist dismissals, compared products, and product search queries sent to the service.
- Uploaded images: product images or label photos you submit for scanning, and profile avatar images if you choose to upload one.
- Feedback and reviews: product feedback, ingredient feedback, ratings, review text, optional notes, and timestamps.
- Product and ingredient context: product names, brand names, ingredient lists, image URLs, product URLs, category information, and analysis results shown in the app.
- Technical information: app language, request metadata, server logs, and IP address information used to operate, secure, troubleshoot, and rate-limit the service.

We do not knowingly collect precise location, contacts, HealthKit records, payment card information, advertising identifiers, or data for cross-app advertising tracking.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Create and maintain your account.
- Analyze skincare product labels and ingredient lists.
- Show skin-type fit, avoided ingredient warnings, product comparisons, and ingredient details.
- Save your scan history, product history, saved products, preferences, and feedback.
- Generate ingredient signals based on your feedback and preferences.
- Improve search, product matching, ingredient analysis, security, reliability, and performance.
- Respond to support requests and comply with legal obligations.

## Third-Party Services

PIBU may use third-party service providers to operate the app, including:

- Google Sign-In for authentication.
- Google AI/Gemini services for product or ingredient analysis when enabled.
- Naver APIs or linked Naver product data for product search, product links, and product images where available.
- Cloud hosting, database, storage, and infrastructure providers.
- Public review or product-information sources when a feature requires product context.

These providers process information according to their own terms and privacy policies. We do not sell your personal information.

## When We Share Information

We may share information:

- With service providers that help us operate PIBU.
- When required by law, legal process, or government request.
- To protect PIBU, users, or others from fraud, abuse, security issues, or harm.
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets.

If you submit a product review, your display name and review content may be visible to other users.

## Your Choices

You can use in-app controls to:

- Update your display name, language, skin profile, and ingredient preferences.
- Delete individual history items or clear scan history.
- Add, update, or remove product feedback.
- Save or unsave products.
- Upload or replace a profile image.
- Delete your PIBU account from Profile.

When you delete your account, PIBU deletes the account record and related scan history, feedback, saved products, watchlist dismissals, reviews tied to your account, and managed profile avatar files, subject to any legal or security retention requirements.

## Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide PIBU, maintain your account, personalize your app experience, protect the service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Some product, ingredient, and analysis records may remain in our general product catalog after account deletion if they are not uniquely tied to your account.

## Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children

PIBU is a general-audience app and is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we collected personal information from a child under 13 without required consent, we will delete it.

## International Processing

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than where you live, including the United States and countries where our service providers operate.

## Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and provide additional notice where appropriate.
